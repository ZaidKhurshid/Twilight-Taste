    Butter Chicken

    Tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, creamy tomato gravy.

    This North Indian classic is one of our most loved dishes — perfectly spiced, buttery, and full of flavor. Served with naan or basmati rice for the ultimate comfort meal.

    Tandoori Platter

    A sizzling mix of grilled chicken, fish, and kebabs straight from the clay oven.

    Marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices, then flame-grilled to perfection. This platter is a feast for your senses — smoky, juicy, and absolutely unforgettable.

    Paneer Tikka Masala

    Cubes of cottage cheese grilled and simmered in spicy tikka masala sauce.

    A vegetarian delight that bursts with flavors. Creamy, tangy, and perfectly balanced, this dish is a go-to for lovers of Indian cuisine with a modern touch.

    Mutton Rogan Josh

    Slow-cooked lamb in a deeply spiced Kashmiri curry.

    This aromatic specialty features tender chunks of mutton simmered with onions, yogurt, and bold spices. A royal dish that's rich in tradition and taste.

    Sizzling Biryani

    Aromatic basmati rice layered with spiced meat or vegetables, cooked in dum style.

    Infused with saffron, herbs, and spices, our biryani is served sizzling hot with raita on the side. A true celebration of flavor and tradition in every bite.
